Welcome to Slice & Spice, where culinary artistry meets unmatched flavor! Founded with a clear vision to serve authentic comfort foods, we specialize in wood-fired artisan pizzas and aromatic smoky party jollof prepared with top-tier local and imported ingredients.

Whether you are joining us for a cozy indoor dinner, hosting a family gathering, or ordering quick deliveries straight to your doorstep, our commitment to exceptional quality shines in every single bite.
